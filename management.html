I would describe myself as a person with infinite curiosity in most things. During my college years, I participated in various clubs, from mountain climbing to fintech studying. Also, I tried to work in different areas -operation, marketing, editor- to know how their works were like. I even took a gap year to explore further, working as a farmer in Germany and promoting a hostel in Thailand, for instance. With those experiences, I know the career I would like to pursue is a position that always has new knowledge to learn and allow me to explore.

Being a contributing editor allows me to explore this world by constantly researching what's happening worldwide and in certain areas. But later, I wish to engage with the things I am interested in on a deeper level, which is about consumer-facing technology.

Celebrating Wedding Online

( product design / web design)

The pandemic has changed the way we live and also how we hold a wedding. Despite the harsh situation, my friend Chin-Chen still remains her plan to hold the wedding.

But due to the timing, her family and friends couldn't fly to Switzerland and celebrate such a big day. So, after talking over the reality we faced, as her bridesmaid, I am in charge of helping her develop an online wedding invitation that allows her beloved person to participate even in the distance.

▍duration: March-May.2021

goal

＞ create the look of the online invitation site.

＞ interview with the guests and find the best platform to gather everyone

process

▍Design Concept

To understand what kind of feeling she would like to present in the invitation, I asked her to collect some inspiration photos and made a mood board. After that and further discussion, we decided to create a Nordic style looking with forest decorations and host it on WordPress.

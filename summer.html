Virtual Summer Vacation

( product design / web design)

The city I lived in is lucky enough not to face lockdown during the pandemic in 2020. However, in May 2021, the covid cases surged, and the government announced the level 3 alert, which means we can still go out, but many places have closed.

During this period, friends and I have to suspend all our plans for the summer. So I organized the online chat time each weekend to encourage each other to stay at home as long as possible. When we talk about all our canceled plans, I have an idea in mind - to recreate the atmosphere of a specific situation or vibe with a background music web page.

▍duration: May.2021-Jun.2021

▶️ stay at home this summer

goal

＞ to know what's the most tempting activities my friends want to do outdoor in this period.

＞ assemble the vibe of those activities online.

process

▍Orgazine a chatting time with my friends

For the friends who want to join this project, I would schedule a weekend time with them and ask them to do a virtual activity like a wine tasting, yoga, or watching a movie together to encourage them not to go out. During this period, I would conduct my survey question about what kind of activities they missed the most outdoors.

▍Collect The Elements

Inspired by a project from a Mexican bar named Maverick, I would ask my friends to share a photo of them doing those activities and create a playlist about what kind of music they feel like listening to while doing it with me. Also, I discussed with them if there's the extra thing they want to put on it.

▍outcome

I continued the project for five weeks and updated a new version of the site each Saturday. The page consists of a paragraph of words from my friends with the playlist they made on the left side. In the middle of the page is a picture I draw about how it would look if they are doing the activities. And the right side presents the background noise it might have in the actual soundings. Combined all the elements, the page recreated a unique vibe that makes people feel like they are in that specific situation.

＞ My frined used to do her horse ridding trainning every summer.

＞ Before the pandemic, my friend does the road trip every summer. This year, we watch Thelma & Louise together at home.

＞ Me and my other college friends used to plan to attend my friend's wedding in Switzerland.

＞ My friend used to plan to move in with her partner and enjoy their summertime together before studying abroad.

＞ me and my friend like to talk about the book we read that week and have a drink together before the pandemic.

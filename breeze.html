"Riding Online Wave"

Breeze Group is an infamous luxury department store in Taiwan. In these two years, they want to expand the business from offline to online. Breeze Daily is established with the anticipation of having the voice of fashion and consumer goods in the young generation.

breeze daily official site

my role

1. create the look of the website with the demand

2. produce CTA button to link with Breeze Online, the online market place of Breeze Group

3. branding and improve SEO for the site

process

starting from scratch

As a web designer, I was hired since the web developer quit the job after building up the site's framework with WordPress. I played the role of displaying the sample site for the editorial board and adjusting the layout according to their demand.

Also, I manage a user guide to help the editorial board understanding site features.

CTA button

In the convenience of letting readers shop the item directly after reading the article, I was in charge of designing the CTA button which connects to Breeze Online.

-challenge

Breeze Online: big button, to attract readers

Breeze Daily: inline small button, to maintain a smooth reading experience

-finding: why big button is better

At first, I designed 16 different buttons for them to choose from, and the picture above shows the type of buttons picked by each team with their concern.

I proposed my research on the button and explained why I think a more prominent button would be better: there's no problem with both buttons on the web page since I made both of them can be hover over and present the clickable appearance. But when it comes to the mobile page, the small underline button will be less identifiable as the studies by the MIT Touch Lab suggest that the button shouldn't be smaller than 10mm x 10mm due to the average size of fingertips.

Google Startup Competition

(Team Leader / Product Design)

I participated in a startup event of Google. My idea of creating the sport streaming app has attracted five members to join me. We made a prototype for the event and won the first prize.

▍duration: 13-15.Oct.2017

process

▍Pitching my idea

I always have interest in the startup, so I participate this event. To convince people to join me, I have done a lot of research and made several ideas before attending. At the event, I pitch the idea of recreating a sports streaming app so that people can enjoy the game from every angle they wish. The idea attracted two software engineers, a hardware engineer, a graphic designer, and a marketing manager.

▍assign the role and make it present

As the team leader, I explained my idea comes from knowing a new type of camera that can record 360 degrees, and I found out that there's a need in a sports game in the market for my team members.

To ensure the idea work, I have several discussion with the engineers, then use a relatively easy understanding term to explain to the graphic designer.

▍selling idea to the judges

By designed an actual prototype of the app and with the beautiful mock-up made by my teammates, I presented it to the judges about how it works? how to use it? and our business model.

In the end we won the first prize.
